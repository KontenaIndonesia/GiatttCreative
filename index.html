How do fees work?

Submit a project on Giattt is free. There are three basic income fees: a income fees, a transaction fee, and a payment processing fee.

Cost range for income fees is 2.5%-10% /income. This applies for Octopus package only. If you submit a ready-to-use goods as projects, we wont take commission. It means, you keep your income 100%. Exclude transaction and payment processing fee at least 5%, and shipping price for creative goods. If you accept Paypal as a payment, there is a payment processing fee based on their fee structure.

All fee are billed in USD. If your bank's currency is not USD, the amount may differ based on changes in the exchange rate.

If you accept payments with PayPal, funds will be deposited into your PayPal account. We encourage sellers to use a PayPal Business account and not a Personal account.
